🛡️ Blokada Family — Cross-Device Parental Controls, Built on Blokada's Trusted Privacy Stack

Keep your kids safe online without forcing your whole family onto the same platform. Blokada Family gives you cross-device parental controls, built on the same open-source ad-blocking and privacy stack that powers Blokada.

✨ Why Blokada Family

• One parent app, many child devices — manage your kids' phones and tablets from one place, regardless of platform.
• App and content blocker — block apps, websites, and entire categories with a tap.
• Live activity stats — see what is being blocked on each device and adjust as you go.
• SafeSearch enforcement — search engines return only age-appropriate, filtered results.
• Customizable per-device rules — fine-tune what is allowed for every child.
• Built-in ad and tracker blocking — every protected device gets the full Blokada experience as a bonus.

🛡️ What it protects against

• Inappropriate or harmful websites and apps
• Adult, gambling, and violent content categories
• Trackers and ads from social-media networks
• Malicious and phishing sites
• Unfiltered, distracting search results

🚀 Easy to set up

1. Install Blokada Family on the parent's device.
2. Add each child's device.
3. Choose the rules — categories, apps, and search filters per child.
4. Watch the live stats and adjust as your family grows.

🌍 Designed for modern families

Built for families with mixed devices and changing needs. One app, every child, full control — whether your kids are on iPhone or Android.

Give your family a calmer, safer internet with cross-device parental controls — without giving up flexibility.
